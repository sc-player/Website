The Immovable Object features a very deep tech tree. The current version has 3 tiers of technology. There are a few different unit types. Besides your character, there are warriors and archers. Warriors can be further upgraded into swordguards or assassins, who specialize in defense and offense respectively. Archers can be upgraded to either fire archers or ice archers, who focus in damage dealing and crowd controlling respectively. There are different types of buildings as well. Technology buildings allow you to train units, research upgrades, and progress to more advanced technology. Income buildings provide you with gold at a steady rate. Walls are used to create barricades to funnel the enemy hordes.
